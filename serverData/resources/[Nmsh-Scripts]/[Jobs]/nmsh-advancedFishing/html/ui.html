Advanced Fishing

  • What is FISHPASS Level?

    A system where you can earn xp by fishing and doing quests and level up accordingly. This system works in direct proportion to the following "Fish Level" system

  • What is Fish Level?

    Each fish has its own level requirement, if the level of the fish is higher than your FISHPASS Level, you can keep the fish (NOTE: Like the fish level, there is also a requirement for rod level that affects the catchability of the fish and these are independent of each other)

  • What is Fishingrod Level?

    Every fish has a specific Fishing Rod Level requirement to be catchable, the fishing rod you are fishing with is the one you need to keep If it is lower than the Angling Requirement Level of the fish you want, you cannot keep the fish.

  • How to Raise The Fishing Rod Level?

    Contact the Fishing Rod Upgrade NPC to upgrade your current fishing rod to the next level fishing rod for a fee. (During this process, depending on your luck, the fishing rod in your hand may break)

  • Tasks and Functioning

    If you finish any mission and receive your reward, all your missions will be reset in an average of 3 days. If you do your missions without interruption, it will help you to increase your FISHPASS level quickly.
    • If you do not use bait, there will be a higher rate of trash caught in your fishing rod..
    If you do not use illegal bait, the endangered fish you try to catch in the illegal area will be caught on your fishing rod. will not get stuck!
    To catch a fish, you must meet both the Fish Level and Fishing Rod Level requirements in the FISHPASS menu.
